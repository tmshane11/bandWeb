The Podunk East Middle School Band currently has 300 members and possess six award-winning ensembles, including our Wind Ensemble, Symphonic Band, Concert Band, Jazz Band, Indoor Drumline, and prestigious beginner program, which have received some of the highest honors in the nation. Since our inception in 1998, the Podunk East Band has fostered traditions of excellence, musicianship, and innovation. The band program is the largest fine arts elective and spirit organization on campus. Our musicians get the chance to perform at concerts, pep rallies, football games, elementary schools, and throughout the community.

Staff

Rick Sanchez

Band Director

Rick Sanchez is the Director of Bands at Podonk East Middle School. During his tenure, the bands at Podonk East have received local, national, and international acknowledgements in concert band. Most recently the Podonk East Wind Ensemble placed 2nd in the Texas State Honor Band Competition. This marks the 30th consecutive year that the Wind Ensemble has placed in the top 4. In the spring of 2015, the Flower Mound Wind Symphony was invited to perform at the 69th Annual Midwest International Band and Orchestra Clinic.

Rick lives in Podonk with his daughter and her husband, while going on miscellaneous adventures with his grandkids. His hobbies including chemistry and dabbling in mechanics.

BoJack Horseman

Associate Director

BoJack Horseman was named Associate Director of Bands at Podonk East Middle School in June of 2009. Prior to his appointment at Podonk. Mr. Horseman served as the Associate Director of Bands at Wapakoneta High School in Wapakoneta, Ohio.

Bands under the direction of Mr. Horseman have received consistent Sweepstakes Awards in University Interscholastic League competition for Marching, Concert Performance, and Sight Reading. In 2004, Mr. Horseman was named as Stark High School's "Teacher of the Year" and honored by the PTA with the life membership award. In 2005, Mr. Horseman was selected for induction into the American School Band Directors Association, which honors outstanding band directors in the United States.

His professional affiliations include the Texas Music Educators Association, Texas Bandmasters Association, Texas Music Adjudicators Association, Tau Beta Sigma and Phi Beta Mu International Band Fraternity. Mr. Horseman maintains an active schedule as a clinician and adjudicator. He holds the Bachelor of Music Education degree from the University of North Texas and a Master of Music Education from the University of American Samoa.
